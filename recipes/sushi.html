Sushi



A beautiful platter of Sushi of various types!


Sushi is one of the most versatile and amazing foods availabile, usually including rice, meat and vegetables!


Ingredients


  • Rice
  • Nori
  • Salmon
  • Teriyaki Chicken
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber

Steps


  1. Make the sushi rice
  2. Chop all ingredients to size
  3. Put sushi rice on nori
  4. Spread over but leave the top inch along the length without rice
  5. Put ingredients on top of the rice at the bottom of the nori
  6. Wet the top of the nori then roll the sushi
  7. Enjoy your Sushi!
